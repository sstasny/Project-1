ABOUT ALPHAS

ALPHAS is a locally owned and operated fitness facility. Our members are our primary focus. Their success is our success. We are a 24/7 gym and provide our members with some of the best fitness equipment available with fitness cardio and circuit machines, Hammer Strength free weights and plate weighted circuit equipment, a multi-purpose area complete with TRX, punching bag, battle rope, medicine balls, etc. ALPHAS isn’t your typical “corporate” style gym. We pride ourselves in an “old school” vibe with a “new school” mentality. Our gym is filled with members from all ranges of fitness levels, from the soccer mom beginner to the advanced, pro athlete. If you are just starting out or are in competition shape, ALPHAS can and will meet your needs.

MEMBERSHIP

Why choose ALPHAS? We are locally owned and operated and cater to our members, we have top of the line equipment, in a comfortable, friendly environment, and we have 24/7/365 access for our members. Don't workout on someone else's schedule, work out on YOUR TIME! At ALPHAS, we try to make joining as quick and easy as possible so you don’t waste time when you want to get on with your workout instead. Simply fill out the online form to pre-register, then sign a single document in person, pay your fees, and you are done! Our membership terms include zero enrollment, no annual fees, and no hidden charges. We have several options to meet the needs of our clients. If you have special terms, or would like information on group or couples rates, please give us a call.
